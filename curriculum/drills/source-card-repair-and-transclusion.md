# Source-Card Repair And Transclusion

Goal: treat source-card mismatch as a first-class repair lane, not a hover-writing problem.

## Principle

Transclusion means one source-card correction should update every quoted/reused instance through regeneration. A
repair decision must list downstream artifacts that become stale: qword denominator, qword crosswalk, hover
candidates, Plan 15 flywheel, curriculum samples, and executor worklists.

## Drill

A source-card row has an owner-supplied source-photo clue, but the displayed qword cannot yet be matched to an
accepted canonical address.

Classify:

- source-card repair first;
- packet-only until accepted;
- sarf/nahw may record internal evidence but must not certify public hover;
- downstream artifacts must be regenerated after accepted repair.

## Answer key

Terminal state is source-card repair or owner packet, not `deploy_ready`. After accepted repair, rerun denominator,
crosswalk, Plan 15, qg projection, and transclusion validators.
