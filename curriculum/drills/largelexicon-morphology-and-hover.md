# Largelexicon Morphology And Hover Drill

Purpose: teach learners why a Qamus hover is contextual, segmented, and
source-clean rather than a dictionary substitution.

## Drill Families

1. **Clitic plus host**
   - Identify the preposition/pronoun piece.
   - Explain why a host-only gloss is incomplete.
2. **Verb prefix, stem, subject, object**
   - Split visible verb pieces.
   - State what each piece contributes.
3. **Derivative and plural pieces**
   - Identify a participle prefix and plural suffix.
   - Explain the difference between a token-form gloss and a root gloss.
4. **Function token**
   - Classify the possible functions.
   - Say what evidence would certify the hover.
5. **No-root discipline**
   - Explain why a proper name or function token can be useful without a root.

These drills use largelexicon candidate rows as teaching material. They do not
claim live Qamus completion and do not expose external evidence labels.
