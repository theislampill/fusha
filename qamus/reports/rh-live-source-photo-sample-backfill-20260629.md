## Scope

rebuild, no service restart, no whitelist append, and no public coverage claim.

## Finding

Several particle source-photo locator fixes had previously been applied directly to
`qamus/indexes/source_photo_entry_locator.json`, which is generated by
`tools/build_source_photo_entry_locator.py`. A later regeneration correctly followed
the generator, but it wiped those direct verified locator edits because they were not
present in the source-owned sample file.

This is a generated-artifact ownership bug: verified source-photo edges must live in
`qamus/reports/source-photo-verified-samples.jsonl`, then the locator must be rebuilt
from that file.

## Change
